Who I Am

About Me

A little bit about my story, what drives me, and how I think about design.

Available for work
Sara Zargan
UI/UX Designer · Based in Ahvaz, Iran 🇮🇷
I'm a UIUX designer with 2+ years of experience crafting digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. I specialize in SaaS products, mobile apps, and design systems turning complex problems into intuitive, pixel-perfect interfaces.
My Story

I started my journey in design through front-end development, where I learned the basics of HTML and CSS and became curious about how digital interfaces are built. During my university studies, I was introduced to UI/UX design, which gradually shifted my focus from coding to user-centered thinking and product experience.

As I explored UI/UX more deeply, I became increasingly interested in user behavior, interaction patterns, and the reasoning behind effective digital products. My first remote role at a design agency was a turning point — it helped me realize that this is the field I truly want to grow in. Each experience since then has sharpened my design thinking and attention to detail.

Today, I focus on improving my skills continuously and creating thoughtful, user-centered digital experiences. I approach every project with curiosity and care, whether I’m refining an existing product or designing from scratch, aiming to build solutions that feel simple, intuitive, and meaningful for users.

How I Work
🔍
User-Centered First
I base my design decisions on user behavior, feedback, and usability principles. I focus on real needs rather than assumptions.
🧩
Design Systems Thinking
I build scalable and consistent UI systems that help teams design and ship products more efficiently across complex interfaces.
🤝
Cross-Team Collaboration
Working in freelance and agency environments taught me how to align design with developers, clients, and business goals.
✨
Craft & Detail
I pay close attention to spacing, hierarchy, and micro-interactions to ensure every detail contributes to a clear experience.

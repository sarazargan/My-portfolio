My Story

I discovered my passion for design during my graphic design studies, where I fell in love with the intersection of aesthetics and usability. What started as a fascination with typography and visual composition gradually evolved into a deep obsession with how people interact with digital products.

Over the years I've worked with startups, agencies, and scale-ups — each experience sharpening my eye for what makes a product truly great. I believe the best design is almost invisible: it just feels right, flows naturally, and makes people's lives a little easier.

Today I focus on building products that are not just visually compelling, but grounded in real user needs and backed by thoughtful research. Whether I'm auditing an existing product or designing from a blank canvas, I bring the same curiosity and care to every pixel.
